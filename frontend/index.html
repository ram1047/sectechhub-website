About TechClick Infosec

Empowering networking professionals with cybersecurity expertise

Founded in 2017, Techclick is dedicated to bridging the gap between networking professionals and cutting-edge cybersecurity knowledge.

We believe that in today's interconnected world, networking expertise must be combined with robust security insights to create truly resilient digital infrastructures.

Our mission is to empower networking professionals with the cybersecurity skills and knowledge they need to protect and secure the networks that power our digital economy. Through comprehensive training programs, expert consulting services, and hands-on practical experience, we help professionals stay ahead of evolving threats and technologies.

At Techclick Infosec, we understand that networking and cybersecurity are not separate disciplines but interconnected fields that require a holistic approach. Our training programs are designed by industry experts who have real-world experience in both networking and security domains.

We offer a wide range of courses, from foundational certifications like CompTIA Security+ to advanced specializations in firewalls, load balancers, zero trust, and ethical hackinging. Our goal is to provide practical, hands-on learning that prepares you for real-world challenges.

Whether you're a seasoned network engineer looking to expand into security or a newcomer entering the field, we're here to support your journey with high-quality training and expert guidance.

Palo Alto Firewall Administrator

Official Training Syllabus

Course Overview

Duration: 5 Days | Level: Intermediate

Fee: ₹25,000

Syllabus

  • • Decryption — SSL Decryption, Certificate Management
  • • High Availability — Active/Passive, Active/Active Clustering
  • • Threat Prevention — WildFire, DNS Security, IoT Security
  • • Logging & Monitoring — Log Forwarding, SNMP, Syslog
  • • Hands-on Labs — Real-world firewall configuration scenarios
